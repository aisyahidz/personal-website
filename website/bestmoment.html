The Best Moment of 2025 — Mama's 53rd Birthday Surprise

It happened at Café Halzan, Penang.I planned everything quietly from the birthday message written on a waffle to the table decorations I coordinated with the café staff. In the middle of dinner, the staff walked toward our table carrying the decorated waffle and began singing Happy Birthday. Mama froze, laughed, and then became speechless, it is the kind of quiet that shows someone is deeply touched.

For me, the most touching part was watching her smile so genuinely. Seeing her happy felt like the truest kind of fulfilment, which a reminder that making your mother smile is one of the purest forms of love.

"If happiness had a shape, it would look like her smile !"
